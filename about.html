The IgniteFC app will help you keep up to date with the Fort Collins gathering of geeks, designers, and hipsters for a night of beer drinking and fun.

This app was developed both to give a little back to the Ignite community, as well as to experiment with various libraries and improve upon various coding methods and it will hopefully be ported to Android and other mobile phones in order to learn each platform better.

Thanks for downloading ;-P
